Our Story

From a single 5K on Marine Drive to a movement reshaping Lucknow's youth fitness culture.

July 2024

First Edition — Elite Marathon '24

Held at Marine Drive, Lucknow on 14 July 2024. The inaugural 5K introduced Elite's vision to the city — a run designed to turn miles into memories. Cash prizes, T-shirts, medals, and hampers for every finisher. Supported by Decathlon Vibhuti Khand, Daylite, and Bonn.

1
5K Run Marine Drive Cash Prizes Decathlon

"What began as a small gathering of 6 runners gradually developed into a steadily growing community. By Week 15, attendance had grown to 103 participants."

2
Sept 2024 – May 2025

Club Season 1

First official club session at Botanical Garden, Lucknow on 22 September 2024 — just 6 runners. Over 15 weekly sessions, the community grew to 103 participants by the final session on 18 May 2025.

June 2025

Second Edition — Elite 5K Run '25

Janeshwar Mishra Park, 22 June 2025. Nearly 250 runners. "Don't scroll your life away. Run it instead." Prime sponsors: Lucknow Falcons. Partners: Decathlon, Knock Sense, Danbro, CM Yuva Yojna, and Mouth Wide Shut bakery for the Top 5 women finishers.

250 Runners Janeshwar Park Lucknow Falcons CM Yuva Yojna

"Not every story we tell is measured in kilometres. Some begin where the usual rhythm breaks — where running shoes pause, curiosity steps in, and a different kind of energy takes over."

July 2025 – Present

Club Season 2 & Beyond

Season 2 opened at Janeshwar Mishra Park on 13 July 2025. The club expanded into new territory with a Pickleball Tournament on 28 December 2025 at Pickleball Café, Mill Road — in collaboration with Athrise India. Movement culture, beyond the miles.
