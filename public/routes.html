The Riverside Collective Loop

This 6.2-mile stretch isn't just about the elevation—it's where the heart of the Elite Run Club beats. Starting at the Old Bridge and weaving through the West-End gardens, this route is famous for its panoramic skyline views and the mid-way water station hosted by local vendors. It's a club favorite because of the shared energy every Tuesday morning.

Moderate Difficulty
